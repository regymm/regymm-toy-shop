Things you should know before...

TLDR: You shouldn't expect too much from a random folk on the Internet.

- Refurbished/Scavenged/Unknown-source-d chips are used in order to provide such a cheap price. Though I'll certainly test everything and have never seen a good chip going bad so far(only ones that come as bad), reliability won't be as good as other well-designed products. So don't have too high an expectation.

- All board contains lead(Pb), so at least wash your hand after touching solder joints, and dispose according to local regulations.

- Details of the product may not be well controlled, including but not limited to how many components are soldered on board(as long as guaranteed functionality), shape and color of board, types of the small bridge boards, visible scratches and wounds(again, as long as it works), and some manually soldered components(my PCB files are too crappy to be properly SMT-ed). But I'll send a picture of what will be shipped.
